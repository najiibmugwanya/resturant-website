ingredients for irish beef stew

  • beef stew
  • olive oil
  • wheat flour
  • peeled carrots, tomatoes
  • onions
  • garlic

    coatbeef cubs in flour then toss them in oiland fry untill golden blown .add the carrots ,potatoes,onions,and garlic. mix together with the beefbroth and tomato cover and cook ,and then simmer to thicken
